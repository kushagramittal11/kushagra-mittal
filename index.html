Profile

Hey there! I am Kushagra Mittal and was born on 11th April 2007 in the metropolitan city of Gurugram in India. I attend the Delhi Public School, in Gurugram, where I have been living since my birth. I enjoy tackling challenges and spend quite a lot of time learning more about interesting topics in science and maths such as quantum mechanics, black holes, special relativity etc, tackling logical puzzles and riddles and composing poems.

I am told I excel in studies, even when I am always busy in pursuing my hobbies. I have been learning to play the guitar since I was eight and am scheduled to appear for Trinity College London's Grade 5 Exam for Plectrum Guitar. In my free time, I listen to songs, read novels (fantasy, sci-fi and mystery, mostly with a touch of humour), even write short stories and poems play football, learning programming and practice guitar.
